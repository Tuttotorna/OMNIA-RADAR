# OMNIA-RADAR — One Page

## What it is

OMNIA-RADAR is a structural detection system.

It scans any sequence and measures:

```text
how much structure is present
and how that structure behaves under perturbation

It works without:

semantics

interpretation

domain knowledge



---

What it is NOT

OMNIA-RADAR does NOT measure:

meaning
truth
coherence
quality
correctness
usefulness

It is not:

a reasoning system

a validator

a decision engine



---

Core Result

RADAR reveals a consistent pattern:

maximum structure ≠ maximum stability

More precisely:

higher structure → higher fragility under perturbation


---

What is new

RADAR separates two dimensions that are usually confused:

structure intensity   (how much structure exists)
structure persistence (how long it survives)

This creates a structural map:

high intensity + low persistence → brittle structure

medium intensity + high persistence → resilient structure

low intensity + flat persistence → noise baseline



---

Why it matters

RADAR allows you to scan:

language

code

data

patterns

unknown systems


as pure structural fields, without asking what they mean.


---

One sentence

OMNIA-RADAR detects structure independently of meaning and shows that the strongest structures are often the most fragile.
